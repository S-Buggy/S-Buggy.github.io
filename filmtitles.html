The Brief
This brief involved designing the film titles for the 4th year film Beautiful Youth. It is about a girl who has a severe mental illness, psychosis, and it covers deep themes like loss, friendship, drug abuse and love.
The Visual Sparkle
The girl is represented by the rose in the film titles below. It is burning to ash, to symbolise her slow falling apart due to her illness. The art direction of the burning roses is the main focus of this piece and it forms the basis of the visual language.

Promotional website and Instagram advert
Promotional booklet
At the screening of the film, a promotional booklet in a box with a rose would be presented to all attending. It would also contain a vimeo password to watch the movie again online, and so the movie could be shared and passed on to others.
