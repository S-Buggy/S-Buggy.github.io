The Brief

Web design

Motion

Animation

The brief for this project was to redesign the Tusla website in a more child-friendly way. Tusla provides support for families and children involved in social care. A lot of user tesing and research was involved.

Share
The Visual Sparkle
The sparkle in this is the friendly character that pops up in places. The concept is to encourage communication in children. They can come to the website and ask any questions they might have. Circles and bright colours are used to appeal to the young audience.

The Landing page
The Landing page
The landing page is the first page the user comes across. The exciting animation is aimed to immediately engage and interest children. They then click on their age group and enter into the home page.
